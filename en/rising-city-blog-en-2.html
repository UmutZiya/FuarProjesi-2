Rising City & Building and Living Fair Ends

The Rising City Building and Living Fair, organized by KFA Fair Organization, a subsidiary of the Bursa Chamber of Commerce and Industry, significantly contributed to the business volume of the real estate and construction sectors. Held at the Bursa Fair Center, the fair hosted more than 15,000 local and international visitors over four days.
